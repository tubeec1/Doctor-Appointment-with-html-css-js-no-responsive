Ururka Horumarinta Bulshada Soomaaliyeed (SSDA)

Waxaan u taaganahay yaraynta dhibaatada bani’aadamka, horumarinta miyiga, iyo kobcinta bulshadeenna annagoo adeegsanayna waxbarasho, nabadda dhisid, iyo mashaariic waara oo guud ahaan Soomaaliya iyo meelaha kale ah.

Akhri Inta Kale

Who We Are

SSDA is a registered Non-Governmental Organization in Somalia and Finland, founded on December 12, 1998, in Helsinki, Finland.

Yaa Nahay

SSDA waa Urur aan dawli ahayn (NGO) oo ka diiwaan gashan Soomaaliya iyo Finland. Waxaa la aasaasay Disembar 12, 1998, magaalada Helsinki, Finland.

General Information

The aim of the organization is to alleviate human suffering following civil strife and natural disasters. SSDA focuses on rural development to reduce hunger, clan warfare, and lack of education, engaging in relief, emergency, recovery, capacity building, monitoring, and evaluation.

Geographical Coverage

SSDA operates primarily in south and central Somalia: Galgadud, Mudug, Hiiraan, Banadir, Lower Shabelle, plus the Finnish Somali community.

Focus Areas

  • Livelihood, WASH
  • Infrastructure rehabilitation (cash-for-work)
  • Youth empowerment (skills training, recreation)
  • Peace building and reconciliation
  • Education enhancement
  • Gender mainstreaming
  • Environment protection
  • Governance and rule of law
  • Rural development (Agro Pastoral)

Vision

SSDA strives for sustainable social, economic, and political development programs in Somalia.

Mission

Work with Somali communities to improve their social, economic, political, and cultural well-being through integrated initiatives in emergency, recovery, capacity building, and economic development.

Macluumaad Guud

Ujeeddada ururka waa in la yareeyo dhibaatada aadanaha ka dib dhibaatooyinka colaadda sokeeye iyo musiibooyinka dabiiciga ah. SSDA waxay diiradda saartaa horumarinta miyiga si loo yareeyo gaajada, colaadaha qabiilka, iyo waxbarasho la’aanta, annagoo ka shaqeyna gargaarka, xaaladaha degdegga ah, soo kabashada, dhisidda kartida, kormeerka, iyo qiimeynta.

Goobaha Shaqada

SSDA waxay si gaar ah uga shaqaysaa koonfurta iyo bartamaha Soomaaliya: Galgadud, Mudug, Hiiraan, Banadir, Shabeellaha Hoose, iyo bulshada Soomaalida Finland.

Meelaha Aan Diiradda Saarno

  • Hab-nololeed (Livelihood), WASH
  • Dib-u-habaynta kaabayaasha (lacag-qabso)
  • Dhallinyarada (tababar xirfadeed, madadaalo)
  • Nabadda dhisid iyo dib-u-heshiisiin
  • Kobcinta waxbarashada
  • Is-dhexgalka jinsiga (Gender mainstreaming)
  • Ilaalinta deegaanka
  • Maamul wanaag iyo sharciga xukunkiisa
  • Horumarinta miyiga (Agro Pastoral)

Aragtideenna

SSDA waxay hiigsanaysaa horumar bulsho, dhaqaale, iyo siyaasad oo waara leh (sustainable) gudaha Soomaaliya.

Hadafkeenna

La shaqaynta bulshada Soomaaliyeed si loo wanaajiyo xaaladdooda bulsho, dhaqaale, siyaasad, iyo dhaqameed annagoo adeegsanayna mashaariic isku dhafan oo ka kooban gargaarka degdegga ah, soo kabashada, dhisidda awoodda, iyo horumarinta dhaqaalaha.
